#### 2.2.1 Strategic Pivot from Classification Models to LLM+RAG

Identified that the existing classification model architecture had hit a ceiling after joining the company. Built the strategic case, proposed and led a 3-sprint PoC, presented results to the CEO, and redirected the team's entire AI strategy from per-client trained models to a retrieval-augmented LLM approach. Designed the parallel-run migration architecture for safe rollout.

#### 2.2.2 Chemical AI — Strategic Product That Opened the Chemical Sector

Shaped and shipped a strategic AI product from concept to production that unlocked the chemical/pharma business segment. Automated chemical synthesis modeling generates complete, LCA-ready product models from a chemical identifier — replacing hours of manual expert work. Designed the end-to-end integration with the data import pipeline including recursive enrichment and a verify-then-publish workflow. Now promoted as a named product capability in go-to-market materials.

#### 2.2.3 Self-Service AI Model Performance Framework

Redesigned the model training and evaluation workflow so implementation teams and clients can assess AI quality independently, removing the AI team as a bottleneck for routine retraining cycles.

#### 2.2.4 Operational Foundation for AI Services

Built governance and operational infrastructure: centralized performance scoreboard, deployment checklists, per-client traceability, documentation, SOPs. The trust layer that makes AI services auditable.

#### 2.2.5 Domain Expertise — Data Import Pipeline & AI Service Architecture

Became the SME on end-to-end data flow: customer upload → transformation → AI prediction → model assembly. This understanding drives better product decisions across all AI services.

#### 2.2.6 Closest Match — AI-Powered Component Substitution for Electronics

Designed and shipped a similarity-based AI fallback for electronics BOMs. When exact component matching fails, the service finds the most technically similar component with available impact data, scores it transparently, and suggests it as a proxy — filling gaps in sustainability assessments automatically.

#### Ongoing: AI Enrichment Services Portfolio

Additional AI services that extend platform automation coverage: product classification enrichment (48,000+ class taxonomy), spend sector prediction for Scope 3 emissions. Each follows the standard pipeline pattern with PRD, integration design, confidence handling, and provenance tracking.

### 3.4 GenAI Consultancy -- RAG Solutions & Autonomous Agents
Parallel role during Payments engagement. Advised EPAM clients on GenAI adoption: RAG solutions, autonomous agents, build vs buy, cost modeling. Stakeholder education on AI capabilities and limitations.

## Part 4: Luxoft -- `4. LUXOFT.md`

### 4.1 Role Overview
10+ years in financial services consultancy. Senior PO (2010-2014): financial reporting, forecasting, analytics, requirements engineering. PM (2014-2021): platform products, global rollouts, team leadership. The PO-to-PM transition story.

### 4.2 Unified Data Upload Platform (Investment Bank)
Platform consolidation: replaced fragmented per-team upload tools with a single unified data ingestion platform. Stakeholder alignment across business units, flexible data model, phased migration.

### 4.3 Liquidity Risk Platform (Global Rollout)
Took a UK-only liquidity risk platform to EU/US/APAC. Regional regulatory variations, configuration-driven architecture, multi-region launch sequencing. Post-2008 regulatory complexity.

### 4.4 Risk Monitoring Framework
Cross-domain risk monitoring: metrics, thresholds, alerting, escalation across market/credit/operational risk. Dashboard and reporting layer, multi-source data integration.

### 4.5 Data-as-a-Service for Hedge Funds
Most entrepreneurial project. Data products as managed service for hedge funds. Team leadership, client development, data pipeline architecture, API design.
