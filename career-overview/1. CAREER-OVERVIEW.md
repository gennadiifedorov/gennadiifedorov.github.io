# Career Overview — Gennadii Fedorov

> Interview-ready career narrative. Written in first person as if explaining to an interviewer.

---

I'm a product leader with over two decades of experience spanning the full spectrum from hands-on software engineering to strategic AI product management. My career has followed a deliberate arc: I started as a software engineer and analyst building enterprise systems, grew into a product owner driving financial platforms at scale, and evolved into a senior product manager leading AI-driven products across consulting and SaaS contexts.

## Early Career -- Enterprise Software (Softline, 2004-2010)

I spent six years building enterprise software -- supply chain management systems, electronic document management, database administration. This gave me a strong technical foundation and an understanding of how large organizations actually use software day-to-day. I progressed from developer to senior software analyst, which was my first step toward product thinking -- translating business needs into system requirements.

## Financial Services Era (Luxoft, 2010-2021)

Over ten years at Luxoft, I moved from senior product owner to product manager, working exclusively with global financial institutions. I owned products spanning regulatory reporting, risk analytics, liquidity management, and data-as-a-service platforms.

Key highlights from this period:

- **Liquidity risk platform global rollout** -- Took a UK-based liquidity risk system and led its expansion across EU, US, and APAC regions. This meant adapting to different regulatory frameworks, onboarding regional stakeholders, and managing a product that had to work consistently across jurisdictions with different requirements.

- **Unified data ingestion for an investment bank** -- Built a centralized data upload platform that replaced fragmented, team-specific upload processes. This was a classic data infrastructure problem: multiple upstream systems, inconsistent formats, and a need for validation and traceability.

- **Data-as-a-Service for hedge funds** -- Stood up a platform that packaged and delivered curated datasets to hedge fund clients, turning internal data capabilities into a revenue-generating product.

- **Risk monitoring framework** -- Designed a monitoring and alerting layer for financial risk metrics, giving trading desks and risk managers real-time visibility.

This period sharpened my skills in stakeholder management across heavily regulated environments, complex data pipeline architecture, and scaling products internationally. I also led teams -- managing product owners and analysts, mentoring junior PMs, and learning how to balance delivery pressure with strategic product thinking.

## Consulting & AI Pivot (EPAM Systems, 2021-2025)

At EPAM I held three product roles that collectively moved me into AI product management:

- **AI Knowledge Management Platform** (2021-2022) -- Led product for a SaaS platform that used machine learning to automate project documentation workflows for engineering and manufacturing firms. This was an AI-powered knowledge base that could extract, classify, and organize technical documents -- reducing the manual effort of maintaining project documentation. This was my first role owning an AI-native product end-to-end.

- **Payments Platform** (2022-2025) -- In parallel, I managed a payments platform for a European bank. I owned the full payments roadmap including mobile payments, internal transfers, and payment processing capabilities. This kept me sharp on traditional product management -- roadmapping, regulatory compliance, cross-team coordination, release management.

- **GenAI Consultancy** (2024-2025) -- In my final year, I shifted fully into AI consultancy. I led generative AI adoption programs for enterprise clients, designed RAG-based (retrieval-augmented generation) solutions, and prototyped autonomous AI agents. This is where I developed deep practical expertise in large language models, prompt engineering, AI evaluation frameworks, and the organizational change management needed to ship AI products in enterprises that aren't AI-native.

## Current: AI Product Leadership (Makersite, 2025-present)

I now lead product for the AI & Data Science team at a product sustainability platform. I own a suite of AI services that automate data enrichment, product modeling, and sustainability analysis -- the intelligent layer that transforms raw product data into complete, analysis-ready digital twins.
